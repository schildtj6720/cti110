Self-Expression and Choice within Art

Art is a hobby that truly knows no bounds. Anyone can call themselves an artist, and the contents of their work has no standard to adhere to. The possibilities are endless. An individual may pour their feelings out onto the canvas in any way they would like. With just the stroke of a brush, the drag of a pen, or the swipe of a gluestick, a person can convey their emotions to the world. There are many different mediums that an artist may use to create, and the sole purpose of this website is to inform you, dear reader, of what those mediums may be.
